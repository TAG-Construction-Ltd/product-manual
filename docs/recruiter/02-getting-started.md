# 2. Getting started

Everything between "I've been told I'm getting access" and "I know my way around" — aim to be through this chapter inside your first half hour.

## 2.1 Your account

Access is by **invitation**: an admin adds you to the team and you receive an email with your sign-in link. There is no self-signup — if you're expecting access and no email has arrived, check spam first, then ask your admin to confirm the address they invited (this is the most common cause).

On first sign-in you'll set your password. Choose something long and unique to TAG — this account can see the personal data of every worker on the platform, and it acts in your name: every verification, edit and message you make is permanently recorded against your identity in the audit trail.

## 2.2 Signing in, and when you can't

Sign in at your dashboard URL with your email and password.

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| Forgotten password | **Forgot password?** on the sign-in screen → reset link to your email. Links are time-limited — use it promptly, and request a fresh one if it's expired. |
| No reset email | Check spam; confirm you're using the invited address; after a few minutes, ask your admin to re-send. |
| "Account locked" or repeated failures | Wait a few minutes and retry with a reset password. Still stuck → your admin, then support. |
| Signed out unexpectedly mid-session | Sessions expire for security; sign back in. If it happens constantly, note what page it happens on and tell support — that detail matters. |

## 2.4 Your first thirty minutes — a suggested path

1. **Dashboard** — read the tiles. How many workers, how many pending verifications, what's in the attention block. That's the state of the world.
2. **Workers** — scan the table. Notice the red/amber tinted rows at the top: that ordering *is* the priority list.
3. **Open one profile** — click any worker. Find the tier badge, the cards section, the compliance summary, the audit trail. This page is where most of your answers live.
4. **Verify** — open the queue and review one card end to end (with a colleague the first time). Nothing teaches the platform faster than one real verification.
5. **Messages** — open the inbox, read one thread, notice the status chips. Don't send anything yet — just see the shape.

After that loop you know where everything is; the rest of this manual is depth.

## 2.5 Two habits to start on day one

- **Trust the queue and the colours.** The platform sorts your work — red rows, the Verify badge, "Needs you" threads. Working those in order beats hunting.
- **Never work around the system.** No verifying from screenshots sent on WhatsApp, no keeping worker details in side spreadsheets "for speed". Everything done in the platform is protected and provable; everything done outside it is your personal liability.
