# 13. Troubleshooting & FAQ

Chapter-specific fixes live at the end of each chapter. This page collects the cross-cutting ones — and the questions workers ask *you* that the platform answers.

## 13.1 Access & session

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| Can't sign in | Reset the password first; confirm you're on the invited email; then admin, then support ([2.2](02-getting-started.md)) |
| Signed out mid-task repeatedly | Note which page it happens on and report it — the detail localises the bug |
| A page looks broken/stale | Hard refresh (++ctrl+shift+r++ / ++cmd+shift+r++); sign out/in; try another browser before reporting |

## 13.2 "The data looks wrong"

| Symptom | Usual truth |
|---|---|
| Worker's change isn't showing | Timing — refresh; app→dashboard sync is quick but not instant |
| Two screens disagree about a worker | One of them is filtered or on an old range — clear filters and compare again; if they still disagree, screenshot both and report |
| A number in a KPI tile "can't be right" | Click the tile — the pre-filtered list *is* the number's working-out |
| A worker/card "disappeared" | Almost always: rejected (awaiting re-upload), expired, or hidden by a filter. The worker's audit trail says exactly what happened ([Chapter 10](10-audit-trail.md)) |

## 13.3 Verification snags

| Symptom | Fix |
|---|---|
| Card locked | Colleague has it open — move on with ++j++, it releases when they finish |
| You approved the wrong card | You can't un-happen it (the trail is honest), but you can fix it: request a re-review/re-upload and record the correction — the trail will show both, which is exactly right |
| Extraction is consistently wrong on one card type | Correct the fields (the system learns from corrections) and report the pattern to support with the card type named |

## 13.4 Questions workers will ask you

| Worker asks | Your answer |
|---|---|
| "Why did my tier drop?" | Open their profile's audit section — it names the cause (expiry, deletion, inactivity). Give them the cause and the fix: "your CSCS ran out — renew and upload it, and you'll climb straight back." |
| "What's my score?" | Workers don't have scores — talk tiers and next actions only. "You're Site Ready; one thing stands between you and Site Strong: your work experience entries." |
| "I uploaded it days ago, why isn't it counted?" | Uploading isn't verifying — check the queue; if it's sitting there, that's your queue discipline talking |
| "My card was rejected unfairly" | Investigate → Card: reviewer, timestamp, reason, in under a minute ([10.7](10-audit-trail.md)) |
| "Delete my old address from the system" | Current data can be corrected (with the change logged); the *history* of changes is immutable by design. Route formal data-protection requests to your admin/support rather than improvising |

## 13.5 When to contact support

Reproducible bugs, data that stays wrong after the checks above, access problems your admin can't solve. Email **support@tagconstructionltd.co.uk** with: what you did, what happened, what you expected, the worker/record involved, and a screenshot. That five-part shape typically halves the round-trips.
