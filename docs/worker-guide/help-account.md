# Help & your account

## Getting help

- **In the app:** message support from the Help section — a real person answers.
- **Email:** **support@tagconstructionltd.co.uk**
- **Quickest results:** say what you were doing, what happened, and what you expected — one line each. A screenshot helps if something looks broken.

For the everyday questions, the answers are already in this guide: [rejected card](cards.md#rejected-fix-it-fast) · [being checked](cards.md#being-checked) · [tier dropped](tier.md#why-tiers-drop) · [share code trouble](setup-checklist.md).

## Notifications

**Settings → Notifications** controls what the app pings you about. Our honest advice: keep **messages** and **card expiry warnings** on whatever else you tune out — those two are the ones that protect your work.

## Your data

Your documents and details are stored securely and shown to recruiters for one purpose: confirming you're qualified and legal to work. You can ask us what we hold about you at any time — email support.

## Changing your phone number

Your number is your login, so update it **before** the old one stops working: Settings → your details, or tell your recruiter. If you've already lost the old number, email support with your name and we'll verify you another way.

## Deleting your account

**Settings → Delete my account.** The app explains exactly what happens to your data before you confirm — read that screen, it's written for you, not for lawyers. Deleting is permanent for your access; some records are kept where the law requires it (employment and payroll rules), and the deletion screen spells that out.

If you're deleting because something's wrong or frustrating — tell support first. Most problems are ten minutes to fix, and we'd rather fix them.
