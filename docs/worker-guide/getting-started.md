# Getting started

## Install the app

Get **TAG Connect** from the Google Play Store on your Android phone. It's free.

## Sign up

1. Open the app and enter your **phone number**.
2. We text you a **code**. Type it in.
3. That's your login — no password to remember, ever. Signing in again later works the same way: your number, a fresh code.

Keep your phone number current — it's your key to the app. If you change numbers, tell your recruiter before the old one goes dead.

## Say yes to the policies

Before anything else, the app shows you two documents: the **Terms** and the **Privacy Policy**. Open both and have a look — they're short, and they explain how we look after your details: what we store, who sees it, and your rights. Tick the box, tap continue.

Plain version of what they say: your documents are stored safely, recruiters see them to confirm you're qualified and legal to work, and you can ask for your data or delete your account whenever you want ([how](help-account.md)).

## What happens next

The app shows your **setup checklist** — four steps that unlock your profile. That's the [next page](setup-checklist.md). The whole setup takes about ten minutes if your documents are to hand, so before you start, grab: your **passport**, your **CSCS card**, and — if you're not a UK or Irish citizen — your **share code** from GOV.UK.

## If sign-up goes wrong

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| No code arrives | Check signal, wait a minute, tap **Resend**. Still nothing — check the number you typed. |
| "Code not valid" | Codes expire fast. Request a fresh one and type it straight away. |
| Wrong number saved | Contact support — **support@tagconstructionltd.co.uk** — and we'll sort it. |
