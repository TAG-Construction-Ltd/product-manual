# Your profile tier

Your tier is your profile's strength badge — one word that tells recruiters how complete and confirmed your profile is. It sits on your home screen, and recruiters see it when they're picking crews.

## The five levels

| Tier | What it means |
|---|---|
| **New Hand** | Just starting — nothing confirmed yet. Every strong profile starts here. |
| **Site Starter** | Your first document has been confirmed by a recruiter. You're on the board. |
| **Site Ready** | Your ID **and** your CSCS are both confirmed. Recruiters searching for available workers usually start from here. |
| **Site Strong** | The full package, all confirmed: ID, a valid CSCS, two more cards, and your work experience written in. This is the tier that gets you picked first. |
| **Site Pro** | Coming later — it'll add confirmed references on top. Nobody's there yet. |

## How to climb

Each of these moves you up when a recruiter confirms it:

1. **Your CSCS card** — the big one. Upload it, get it confirmed.
2. **Your ID** — passport confirmed (plus your share code where that applies).
3. **Two more cards** — plant tickets, safety certs, whatever you hold.
4. **Your work experience** — three past jobs written in ([how](profile.md)).
5. **Your skills** — a few lines about what you do.

Notice the pattern: **uploading alone never moves you — confirmation does.** A photo sitting "being checked" counts the moment it clears. And there are no shortcuts: the tier can't be bought, blagged, or bumped — which is exactly why recruiters trust it, and why yours is worth building.

One rule worth knowing: **without a valid CSCS you can't reach Site Strong**, no matter how full the rest of your profile is. Keep that card alive.

## Why tiers drop { #why-tiers-drop }

Tiers move both ways. If yours dropped, it's one of these:

| What happened | The fix |
|---|---|
| **A card ran out** — usually the CSCS | Renew it, upload the new one. Once confirmed, you climb straight back. |
| **A document was removed** from your profile | Re-add a confirmed card and you're back. |
| **You've been quiet a while** — months with no profile activity | Any update wakes it up — renew a card, add experience, touch your details. |

A drop is never a punishment and never permanent — it just means the profile stopped reflecting a fully-confirmed picture. The app tells you the reason, and the reason is always the fix.

## What recruiters see

Recruiters see your tier, your confirmed cards, and your experience — the full professional picture. They do **not** see any number or score; the tier *is* the summary. If a recruiter mentions your tier, this page is what they're talking about.
