# TAG Connect — Worker's guide

Short pages, one job each. Find your question, read one page, done. *(ਪੰਜਾਬੀ ਵਿੱਚ ਛੇਤੀ ਆ ਰਿਹਾ ਹੈ — Punjabi version coming soon.)*

## 1 · What this app does for you

Your cards, your ID, your experience — all in one place, checked and confirmed, ready to show any recruiter. Keep it up to date and work finds you faster.

## 2 · Install & sign up

Get **TAG Connect** from the Play Store. Sign up with your phone number — we text you a code, you type it in. That's the whole login. No passwords to forget.

## 3 · Say yes to the policies

Before you start, read and accept the Terms and the Privacy Policy — they open right in the app. They cover how we look after your details.

## 4 · Your setup checklist

Four steps and you're in:

- [x] Identity uploaded
- [ ] Right to Work confirmed
- [ ] Personal details complete
- [ ] First card uploaded (CSCS)

Tick all four and your **profile tier** appears.

## 5 · Upload your ID

Tap **Add** on Identity. Photograph your passport — good light, flat on a table, all four corners in the shot.

## 6 · Right to Work

**UK or Irish?** Just tap **Confirm** — done. **Everyone else:** enter your share code from GOV.UK. Codes run out after a while — if yours is rejected, get a fresh one at gov.uk/prove-right-to-work.

## 7 · Your personal details

Address, National Insurance number, the basics. Two minutes, tap **Complete**.

## 8 · Upload your first card

Tap **Add** on First card and photograph your CSCS. The photo tips that stop rejections: **good light · flat on a table · whole card in frame · no glare**. Both sides if asked.

<div class="screenshot-placeholder">SCREENSHOT wg-08-card-upload.png — the card photo screen with the frame guide</div>

## 9 · "Being checked" — what it means

A recruiter is confirming your document is real and readable. **Nothing more for you to do.** It usually takes about a day. When it clears, your tier moves.

## 10 · Card rejected? Fix it fast

The app tells you exactly why — usually the photo. Read the reason, retake (better light, flat, no glare), tap **Re-upload**. It goes straight back to be checked.

<div class="screenshot-placeholder">SCREENSHOT wg-10-rejected.png — a rejected card with the reason and Re-upload button</div>

## 11 · Your profile tier

Five levels, bottom to top: **New Hand → Site Starter → Site Ready → Site Strong → Site Pro**. It's your profile's strength badge — recruiters see it when they're picking crews. Every strong profile starts at the bottom; verified cards move you up.

## 12 · Climbing up

What moves you: **verified cards** (especially a valid CSCS — nothing beats it), your **ID confirmed**, your **work experience** written in, your **skills** filled out. Each one your recruiter confirms pushes you higher.

## 13 · Keep your cards in date

A card that runs out drags your tier down — the app warns you before it happens. When you renew a card in real life, photograph and upload the new one straight away.

## 14 · Messages from your recruiter

Yellow cards in Messages are things to **do**, not just read — one big button takes you exactly where you need to go. Tap it, do it, done.

## 15 · Add your experience & skills

In your profile: add your past jobs (site, role, dates — three or more is the magic number) and a few lines about what you do. This is what pushes you to the top tier.

## 16 · Get help / delete your account

Questions: message support in the app or email **support@tagconstructionltd.co.uk**. Leaving: **Settings → Delete my account** — it explains what happens to your data before you confirm.
