# TAG Connect — Worker's guide

This guide covers everything in the TAG Connect app — from installing it to reaching the top profile tier. Every page is short and to the point. Find your question in the list, read that page, done.

*(ਪੰਜਾਬੀ ਵਿੱਚ ਛੇਤੀ ਆ ਰਿਹਾ ਹੈ — Punjabi version coming soon.)*

**What the app does for you, in three lines:** your cards, your ID and your experience live in one place, checked and confirmed by a real recruiter. Recruiters picking crews see a profile they can trust. The stronger and more up-to-date your profile, the faster work finds you.

- [Your profile tier](tier.md) — the five levels, how to climb, why tiers drop
- [Your profile](profile.md) — personal details, work experience, skills
- [Messages](messages.md) — talking to your recruiter, the yellow action cards
- [Help & your account](help-account.md) — getting help, notifications, leaving
